Axolotl's Answers
Social question-and-answer website
Welcome to Axolotl's Answers, Q&A service founded in Mykolaiv, Ukraine by four CS students from Petro Mohyla Black Sea National University as a project for one of the disciplines. Axolotl's Answers is a place of knowledge, a real treasue for those ones, who likes diving deep into the well of info from every topic they're interested in. The most efficient path to figuring out the way our world works lies through communication, and that's why we offer you to join Axolotl's Answers and become one of those smart and sociable axolotls, inhabiting this service.
